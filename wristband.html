Why Wristbands for Elders?

For the elderly, maintaining health is critical. Our wristbands not only track health metrics like heart rate, blood pressure, and sleep, but also provide vital alerts in case of emergencies, empowering seniors to stay independent and healthy.

Shocking Fact: A recent report by WHO revealed that elderly individuals with chronic diseases are at a 40% higher risk of health emergencies without real-time monitoring.

How Our Wristband Helps

  • Health Monitoring: Tracks heart rate, BP, SpO2, and sleep patterns for proactive health management.
  • Emergency Alerts: Sends instant alerts to caregivers if there are abnormalities in vital signs.
  • Activity Tracking: Tracks daily steps, activity levels, and provides motivation to stay active.
  • Medication Reminders: Ensures the elderly never miss their medication schedule.
