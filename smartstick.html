Ensuring Safe Movement and Independence

Why Smart Sticks for Elders?

As India’s elderly population grows, ensuring their safety while maintaining their independence is crucial. Falls are one of the leading causes of injury among elderly people. A smart stick equipped with sensors and GPS can help mitigate these risks and provide real-time assistance.

Shocking Fact: According to a study by the World Health Organization, falls are the second leading cause of accidental deaths globally, with the elderly being at highest risk.

How Our Smart Stick Helps

  • Fall Detection: Automatically alerts caregivers if the user falls, sending their GPS location for immediate assistance.
  • Obstacle Detection: Alerts users of nearby obstacles or hazards, reducing the risk of injury.
  • Location Tracking: GPS enables family members to track the user's location in real-time.
  • Emergency Alerts: Sends automatic alerts to family members or emergency contacts in case of health emergencies.
