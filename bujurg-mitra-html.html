Features

Real-time Vitals

Monitor blood pressure, heart rate, oxygen levels and temperature in real-time with immediate alerts for abnormal readings.

Activity Tracking

Monitor daily movements, sleep patterns, and activity levels to ensure healthy lifestyle and detect unusual behaviors.

Fall Detection

Advanced sensors automatically detect falls and immediately alert caregivers or emergency services with precise location data.

Medication Reminders

Timely alerts for medication schedules, ensuring proper dosage and adherence to prescribed treatments.

SOS Button

One-touch emergency assistance connects seniors directly to caregivers or emergency services when needed.

GPS Tracking

Real-time location monitoring provides peace of mind for family members and helps in emergency situations.

About Bujurg Mitra

Bujurg Mitra (which means "Elder's Friend" in Hindi) is committed to improving the lives of seniors by implementing innovative technological solutions which promote independency, safety, and well-being.

Our objective is to provide seniors and their families a peace of mind by developing intuitive, dependable monitoring devices that are simple to use and unobtrusive.

Our team, comprised of healthcare professionals and technology experts, combines medical knowledge with cutting-edge technology to meet the unique issues that the elderly encounter.

We believe that aging doesn't mean giving up independence or dignity. Our products are designed to seamlessly integrate into seniors' lives, providing the support they need while respecting their autonomy.
