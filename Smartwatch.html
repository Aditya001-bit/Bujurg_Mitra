Why Smart Watches for Elders?

India's elderly population is projected to reach 192 million by 2031 (Source: The Hindu). With rising health risks, isolation, and emergency response delays, it's crucial to implement smart monitoring solutions that act proactively rather than reactively.

Shocking Fact: According to a report by NITI Aayog, nearly 50% of elderly Indians live with chronic diseases like diabetes, BP, or heart ailments — yet access to continuous monitoring is negligible.

How Our Smartwatch Helps

  • Real-Time Vitals Tracking: Monitors pulse, BP, oxygen (SpO2), temperature.
  • Abnormality Alerts: Sends auto-alerts to caregivers/family via SMS in case of danger.
  • Medication Reminders: Helps manage daily meds without missing a dose.
  • Location Tracking: GPS enables location detection for wandering elders.
  • Integrated Apps: Sync with health apps, calendar reminders, doctor appointments.
